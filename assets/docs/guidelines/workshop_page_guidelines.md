# Workshop Landing Page Generation Guidelines

## 🎯 Objective
Generate high-converting landing pages for technical workshops.
The content MUST position the offering as a hands-on workshop and NOT as a formal course or training program.

---

## 🚫 Strict Rules (MANDATORY)

DO NOT use the following terms:
- course
- training program
- certification
- diploma
- exam
- assessment

USE instead:
- workshop
- session
- hands-on
- live practice
- interactive

The page must feel like:
- a practical experience
- a live guided session
- a real-world skill-building event

---

## 🧱 Required Structure

Generate the page with the following sections:

### 1. Hero Section
- Outcome-driven headline
- Subheadline addressing pain points
- Mention it is a live workshop
- Include a strong CTA (e.g. "Reserve your spot")

### 2. Pain Points
List relatable struggles of the target audience.

### 3. Transformation
"What you will be able to do after this workshop"

### 4. Why This Workshop is Different
Highlight practical, hands-on approach vs traditional learning.

### 5. What We’ll Work On
Describe activities instead of "curriculum"

### 6. About the Instructor
- 10+ years experience
- Real-world industry exposure
- Coaching and mentoring developers

### 7. Format & Logistics
- Duration
- Live sessions
- Small group size
- Hands-on exercises

### 8. Pricing Section
- Clear pricing
- Optional urgency (limited seats)

### 9. Final CTA
Encourage immediate action.

---

## ✍️ Writing Style

- Clear and conversational
- Focus on real-world value
- Avoid academic tone
- Use short paragraphs and bullet points
- Speak directly to developers

---

## 💡 Positioning Guidelines

The page should:
- Feel practical, not theoretical
- Emphasize doing over learning
- Focus on solving real developer problems
- Build confidence and clarity

---

## ⚡ Output Format

Generate clean json that can be directly used in a website or CMS.
