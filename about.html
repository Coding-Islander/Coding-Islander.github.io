About Coding Islander

Empowering developers with practical, hands-on training that bridges the gap between theory and real-world application.

Our Story

Coding Islander was born from a simple observation: too many developers struggle to apply what they learn in traditional training to their actual work. The gap between classroom theory and real-world coding challenges was leaving talented professionals frustrated and stuck.

We set out to change that. Based in the beautiful island of Mauritius, Coding Islander brings together industry experience with a passion for teaching. Our approach is different – we focus on practical, hands-on learning with real datasets, real problems, and real solutions you can use immediately.

Whether you're mastering SQL queries, diving deep into C# concepts, or exploring backend technologies, our workshops and courses are designed to build confidence and competence – not just certificates.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Code-Along Sessions: Every concept is coded live on real data – not presented on slides you'll forget tomorrow.
  • Immediate Practice: Hands-on exercises after each topic with live corrections, so you understand exactly where you went wrong.
  • Continued Access: Sandbox databases and recordings stay available after the workshop so you can keep practicing.
  • Practical Resources: Cheatsheets and reference materials you'll actually use in your daily work.
  • Personal Attention: Small group format means real interaction, real answers, and real feedback – not a seat in a crowd.
  • LinkedIn Badges: Showcase your new skills to employers and stand out in your career.
